African Grey Parrot

Scientific Name: Psittacus erithacus

Habitat: Rainforests of West and Central Africa

Diet: Fruits, seeds, nuts, and berries

Behavior: Known for exceptional talking ability and intelligence. Highly social and playful.

Interesting Fact: Considered one of the most intelligent bird species in the world.

Sulphur-Crested Cockatoo

Scientific Name: Cacatua galerita

Habitat: Australia and nearby islands

Diet: Seeds, roots, berries, and nuts

Behavior: Very loud and social; often seen in flocks. Can mimic human voices and sounds.

Interesting Fact: Recognized by their distinctive yellow crest, which rises when they are excited or alarmed.

Blue Gold Macaw

Scientific Name: Ara ararauna

Habitat: South American tropical forests

Diet: Nuts, fruits, and seeds

Behavior: Very affectionate and intelligent, with the ability to mimic sounds. Known for their strong pair bonding.

Interesting Fact: Their bright blue and yellow feathers make them one of the most striking birds in the parrot family.
